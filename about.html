The Aegis Knight - 5 : The Lost Legend

Have you tried the new adventure game called "The Aegis Knight - 5 : The Lost Legend"? The game that was awarded as the Game Of The Year by IGN? If not! This is the site where you will get all the information you need before trying out the newest version of "The Aegis Knight"!

Basic Information : The First of "The Aegis Knight" was created in 1999 by our genius game developer, Bryce Svolveyo. This MMORPG game was based on the story of a dog who saved humanity from a war between angel and demon that took place in the dog world. This dog was born half angel half demon and travel to both heaven and hell to seek his true past. Eventually, the dog realize the angels and demons were constantly at war which also brought destruction to the human world. He was determined to stop this long war and save dog nation from brink of extermination.
